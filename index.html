The Random Trivia Game is my first game with a leaderboard, and my first game that stores data on a text file. Includes 30 questions of random trivia I found online. Play until you get one wrong, at which point the game is over. If you're good enough, this is when you're added to the leaderboard.

MapGen 4 is my latest and most advanced map-generation project, and the successor to MapGen 3 and Karel the Cartographer v1.7. It has customizable sizes and colors, and generates land, sand, rock, and water of varrying depths. It uses Python Turtle for its graphics.

Exorcist is a text-based adventure game that follows your character through a strange building filled with stranger creatures. Fight to survive, escape the building, and attain a perfect score. No controls are used, you answer questions to choose the course of your adventure.

The Devil in Me is an image-based game where you can kill enemies, switch modes, and heal, all for the goal of achieving the highscore (which is unfortunately not listed in-game). This game uses the arrow keys to move, click to shoot, W to heal, and S to use your special.
